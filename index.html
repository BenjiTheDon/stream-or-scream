Synopsis

This vision explores a modern, commercial sleek horror aesthetic that captures blurred realism with the tension and paranoia within a technologically driven narrative. When your audience becomes your demon, streaming isn’t entertainment it’s survival. Stream or Scream is a genre-bending short film that captures the dark intersection of fame, technology, and fear.

A modern commercial horror short blending realism and tech horror.

“GO LIVE... OR DIE OFFLINE.”
